## Generating your own courses

With a free Coursify account you can generate courses on any topic you choose using Flash mode — free accounts include 5 AI generations per month. Your generated courses are saved automatically to your personal library on the dashboard, visible only to you unless you choose to publish them.

<Note>
  Upgrade to Pro for unlimited generations, [Audio speak mode](/features/rich-reader), and [Export to Markdown & PDF](/features/rich-reader). See the [plans page](/billing/plans) for details.
</Note>

For a full walkthrough of the generation process — including mode selection, topic tips, and generation limits — see the [Course Generation guide](/features/course-generation).

## Course structure

Every Coursify course, whether public or personally generated, follows a consistent structure so you always know what to expect:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Difficulty badge" icon="gauge">
    A clear **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, or **Advanced** label so you can self-assess fit before diving in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Estimated duration" icon="clock">
    A time estimate — from hours to weeks — so you can plan your study schedule realistically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Section count" icon="layers">
    The total number of lessons across all modules, giving you a sense of the course's depth and breadth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="What you'll learn" icon="graduation-cap">
    A curated list of key outcomes at the top of every course, so you understand the skills and knowledge you'll gain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prerequisites" icon="list-checks">
    Where applicable, a list of assumed knowledge or recommended prior courses to help you decide if you're ready.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Module-organized outline" icon="map">
    All lessons are grouped into thematic modules, letting you navigate the course non-linearly or work through it start to finish.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
