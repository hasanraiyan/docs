The [Coursify dashboard](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/dashboard) is your command center for everything learning-related. From here you generate new courses, revisit your saved library, browse your generation history, and discover featured public content — all in one place.

<Note>
  You need a free Coursify account to access the dashboard, generate courses, and save content to your personal library. [Create a free account](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up) to get started.
</Note>

## Generating from the dashboard

To create new content, use the generation panel at the top of the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter a topic">
    Type any subject, concept, or university syllabus into the topic input field. Be as specific or as broad as you like — Coursify's AI agents handle the research regardless.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a mode">
    Select the generation mode that fits your goal using the mode switcher:
    - **Research** — generates a single, in-depth, source-backed article on your topic.
    - **Flash** — produces a condensed, quick-reference study guide. Free accounts include 5 Flash generations per month.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click the generate button. Coursify dispatches autonomous AI agents in the background to scan verified web sources, organize facts, and write structured content. Depending on the topic and mode, generation typically takes a few seconds to a minute.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once generation completes, the new course or article is saved automatically to your personal library and your generation history.

<Tip>
  Upgrade to the Pro plan to unlock unlimited course generations, Audio speak mode, and Export to Markdown & PDF. See the [plans page](/billing/plans) for details.
</Tip>

## Your library

Your library displays every course and research article you have generated or saved. Each card shows:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Course title" icon="book-open">
    The title of the generated course or article, exactly as Coursify named it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Difficulty level" icon="bar-chart">
    A badge indicating whether the content is **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, or **Advanced**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Section count" icon="list">
    The total number of lessons or sections so you can gauge the scope at a glance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Last updated date" icon="calendar">
    When the course was generated or last modified, helping you track recent activity.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Click any course card to open the rich reader and resume learning exactly where you left off.

## Generation history

The generation history panel is a chronological log of every course and research article you have created. Each entry records:

- **Content title and type** — whether it was a Flash generation or a Research article.
- **Generation date and time** — so you can pinpoint when you created it.
- **Generation count** — how many of your monthly free generations you have used, useful for staying within your plan limits.

Use the history panel to quickly find and reopen content you generated days or weeks ago, even if you have not saved it prominently in your library.

## Featured public courses

The lower section of the dashboard surfaces **featured public courses** — hand-picked courses made freely available to everyone. You can read any featured course in full without an account.

These courses cover high-demand topics and are refreshed as new content is added to the library. If no featured courses are shown yet, check back soon as the library grows.
