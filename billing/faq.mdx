This page answers the most common questions about Coursify's plans, how AI course generation credits work, the difference between Flash and Deep-Research Pro modes, and what to expect when Pro and Scholar plans become available.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do AI credits and course generation work?" icon="circle-help">
    Each course you generate on the Free plan uses one of your five monthly credits. Credits reset automatically at the start of each calendar month — you don't need to do anything to renew them.

    Pro plan members receive unlimited course generations with no monthly cap. If you're on the Free plan and run out of credits before the month resets, you can still read all existing public courses in the course browser.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel or change my plan anytime?" icon="repeat">
    Plans are flexible. Pro and Scholar are not yet available, so there's nothing to cancel or upgrade to right now. Once paid plans launch, you'll be able to manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

    On the Free plan, there's no subscription to cancel — your account simply stays active with the five monthly generation credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the difference between Flash and Deep-Research generation?" icon="flask-conical">
    Coursify offers two generation modes:

    - **Flash** is available on the Free plan. It uses the standard web research engine to generate courses quickly. It's ideal for broad overviews, introductions to new topics, and everyday study.
    - **Deep-Research Pro** is a Pro plan feature. It uses advanced AI models to perform deeper, multi-source research, producing more comprehensive courses with richer explanations and more thorough coverage of complex subjects.

    Both modes generate structured, university-level courses with modules, sections, quizzes, and flashcards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer a discount for students or educators?" icon="tag">
    No student or educator discount has been announced at this time. If you have a question about access for your school, institution, or research group, reach out to support at [raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com](mailto:raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com) and we'll do our best to help.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my free plan generation limit?" icon="gauge">
    Once you've used all five of your monthly Free plan generations, you won't be able to generate new courses until your credits reset at the start of the next month — or until you upgrade to Pro when it becomes available.

    You can still access and read all public courses in the course browser at any time, even after your credits are exhausted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my courses?" icon="download">
    Export to Markdown and PDF is a Pro plan feature. Free plan users can read their generated courses in the Coursify browser, including all modules, sections, quizzes, and flashcards.

    When Pro launches, export options will let you take your course content anywhere — into note-taking apps, documents, or offline reading.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for Pro?" icon="clock">
    Pro is coming soon and no free trial has been announced yet. The best way to get early access is to [sign up for a free account](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up) now. Free members will be first in line when Pro launches.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own provider API key?" icon="key">
    Yes. All plans support saving a provider API key in your account settings. When you save a key, Coursify uses it for AI generation instead of the default service key. This is useful if you already have API access through another provider or want to use a specific model.

    You can add, update, or remove your saved provider key at any time from your account settings. Removing a key switches generation back to the default service key for your plan tier.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
