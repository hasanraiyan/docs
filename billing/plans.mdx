## Generation modes by plan

Coursify offers two research and generation modes depending on your plan:

- **Flash (Free plan)** — Uses the standard web research engine to pull together course content quickly. Great for broad introductions and everyday learning.
- **Deep-Research Pro (Pro plan)** — Uses advanced AI models to perform deeper, more comprehensive research across multiple sources. Produces richer explanations, more complete citations, and more thorough coverage of complex topics.

<Tip>
  Even on the Free plan, Flash-generated courses are structured at a university level with modules, sections, quizzes, and flashcards built in.
</Tip>

## Bring your own provider key

All plans support saving your own provider API key in your account settings. When a provider key is saved, Coursify uses it for AI generation in place of the default service key. You can add, update, or remove your provider key at any time from your account settings without affecting your plan tier or generation credits.
