# Support

Get help with Coursify accounts, course generation, waitlist access, saved keys, privacy, and data deletion.

Last updated: June 14, 2026

## Contact

Email [raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com](mailto:raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com) for support. Include your account email, a short description of the issue, and screenshots or links when helpful.

## Common requests

- Account access or sign-in problems.
- Course generation, queue, or credit issues.
- Saved provider key setup and removal.
- Privacy, terms, and data deletion questions.
- Waitlist or early-access questions.

## Data deletion

To delete your account or personal data, follow the instructions on the [Data Deletion](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/data-deletion) page.

## Response time

Coursify is an independent product. We try to respond as quickly as possible, and priority is given to account access, billing, privacy, and security requests.
