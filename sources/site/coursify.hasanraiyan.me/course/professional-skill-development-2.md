A comprehensive course designed for end-term exam preparation, focusing on professional communication, public speaking, group discussions, and non-verbal communication strategies based on previous year questions and expert notes.

### What you'll learn

- Understand the elements and importance of effective public speaking.
- Master the principles and evaluation criteria of Group Discussions.
- Apply non-verbal communication techniques including kinesics and occullesics.
- Plan, structure, and deliver professional presentations with confidence.
- Analyze and answer previous year exam questions effectively.
