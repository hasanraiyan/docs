# Delete your Coursify data

Use this page to understand what can be deleted and how to request account or data deletion.

Last updated: June 14, 2026

## What you can delete

- Your Coursify account profile and authentication-linked application data.
- Courses, modules, sections, research notes, generation history, and related drafts.
- Saved provider API keys, tool keys, and user-specific configuration.
- Waitlist records associated with your email address.

## How to request deletion

Send an email to [raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com](mailto:raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com) from the email address connected to your Coursify account. Use the subject line "Coursify data deletion request" and include the account email you want deleted.

## Verification

We may ask you to verify account ownership before deleting data. This protects users from unauthorized deletion requests.

## Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days. Some records may remain temporarily in backups, security logs, or fraud-prevention records where retention is required for operational, legal, or security reasons.

## Before deleting

Deletion may be permanent. Export or save any course or research content you want to keep before submitting a deletion request.
