Anyone can browse public courses and read research articles on Coursify without signing in. The moment you want to **generate your own courses**, save them, track your quiz history, or unlock Pro features, you need an account. This page walks you through creating and configuring yours.

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Navigate to [coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up) in your browser. You'll find the account creation form along with a summary of what your free account includes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign up">
    Complete the sign-up form and submit. No credit card is required to create a free account. You're signing up for the Free tier, which includes 5 Flash course generations per month and access to quizzes and assessments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start generating">
    Once your account is active, head back to the homepage or your dashboard to submit your first topic. Your generation quota is immediately available — no waiting period or email confirmation gate before you can start.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What your account gives you

A Coursify account unlocks a set of features that aren't available to guest browsers:

- **Course generation quota** — 5 Flash generations per month on the Free plan; unlimited on Pro
- **Research article generation** — generate standalone, source-backed deep-dive articles on any topic in addition to full courses
- **Saved course library** — every course you generate is saved to your personal dashboard and accessible any time
- **Generation history** — review all past generations, re-open previous courses and articles, and pick up where you left off
- **Interactive quiz generation** — AI-generated multiple-choice assessments are created for each of your courses so you can test your retention
- **Export to Markdown & PDF** *(Pro)* — download your courses as portable Markdown files or clean, print-ready PDFs
- **Audio read-aloud** *(Pro)* — listen to any course with the built-in speak mode
- **Shared workspace notebooks** *(Scholar)* — collaborate with classmates or colleagues in shared notebooks for co-generation, annotation, and group study
- **Provider API keys** — optionally connect your own AI provider keys in account settings (see below)

## Provider API keys (Bring Your Own Key)

You can optionally save your own AI provider API keys in account settings. This "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) model lets you connect integrations tied to providers you already use or have credits with.

- **Where to add them** — navigate to your account settings page and look for the API Keys section. Enter the key for the provider you want to connect.
- **How they're used** — saved keys are only used for features you explicitly activate. Coursify never uses your keys in the background or for other users' requests.
- **Removing a key** — go back to the API Keys section in settings at any time and delete any key you've saved. Removal takes effect immediately.

<Note>
  BYOK keys are optional. Coursify's standard generation engine works without any provider keys on both Free and Pro plans. Keys simply unlock specific user-selected integrations.
</Note>

## Managing your account

**Editing or deleting generated content**

Open any course in your library to access its management options. You can rename, re-generate, or permanently delete any course you own. Deleted courses are removed from your library and cannot be recovered.

**Removing saved API keys**

Visit your account settings page at any time to view and remove any provider keys you've stored. Removing a key immediately disables the integration it was powering.

**Requesting account or data deletion**

To request full deletion of your account and all associated data, visit the [Data Deletion page](/support/data-deletion) and submit a request. Coursify will process your request in accordance with applicable data protection requirements.

<Warning>
  Keep your sign-in credentials secure and do not share your account access with others. If you believe your account has been compromised — for example, if you notice unexpected course generations or changes to your settings — contact Coursify support immediately via the [Help & Support page](/support/getting-help) so the team can investigate and secure your account.
</Warning>
