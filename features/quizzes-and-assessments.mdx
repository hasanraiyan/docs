Active recall is one of the most evidence-backed ways to strengthen long-term retention. Instead of passively re-reading notes, testing yourself on material forces your brain to retrieve information — and retrieval practice is far more effective than highlighting or summarising alone. Coursify bakes this principle directly into the learning experience by automatically generating a quiz for every course section, so you can verify your understanding the moment you finish reading.

## How quizzes work

Coursify generates quizzes dynamically from the content of each course section. There's nothing to configure, import, or create — as soon as a course section is generated, its quiz is ready.

Each quiz is:

- **Multiple-choice** — each question presents one correct answer alongside plausible distractors
- **Section-scoped** — questions are drawn from the material covered in that specific section, not the whole course
- **Instantly graded** — you get your result and an explanation immediately after submitting your answers
- **Automatically regenerated** — because questions are generated from course content, they stay relevant even if the underlying content is refreshed

You don't need to manage a question bank or import anything from a third-party tool. Coursify handles question generation entirely from the course material it wrote.

## Taking a quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a course section">
    From the course overview page, click any section in the sidebar module list to open it in the rich reader. You can also navigate to your course from the [dashboard](/library/dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read through the content">
    Work through the section content — headings, explanations, code examples, and formulas — before attempting the quiz. The quiz tests comprehension of what you just read, so reading first gives you the best signal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the quiz block at the end of the section">
    Scroll to the bottom of the section. The quiz block appears after the main content, clearly labelled with the number of questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer the multiple-choice questions">
    Select your answer for each question and submit. Take your time — there's no timer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See instant results and explanations">
    Coursify marks each answer immediately and shows an explanation for why the correct answer is right (and, where helpful, why the distractors are wrong). Use the explanations to close any gaps before moving to the next section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Flashcards

Alongside quizzes, Coursify includes **flashcards** for reinforcing key terms and definitions. Flashcards are available on the Free plan and complement the quiz experience — quizzes test applied comprehension, while flashcards build rapid-fire recall of vocabulary, formulas, and concepts.

Together, quizzes and flashcards give you two distinct retrieval modes: open-ended recognition through flashcards, and structured comprehension testing through multiple-choice quizzes.
