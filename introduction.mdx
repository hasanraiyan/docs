Instead of piecing together scattered articles or watching hours of videos, you submit a subject — or paste in a syllabus — and Coursify's autonomous research agents scan verified web sources, build a coherent outline, and write every module and section for you. The result is an interactive, richly formatted course you can read, quiz yourself on, and return to any time.

## What Coursify does

Coursify compresses hours of research and note-taking into a repeatable three-step flow.

**1. Submit a topic or paste a syllabus**

Type any subject — from "Basics of Machine Learning" to "Quantum Thermodynamics" — or paste a full university syllabus directly into the generator. You also choose your generation mode: Flash for quick results, or Research for a deeper, Pro-powered deep dive.

**2. AI deep scan researches and builds the outline**

Once you submit, Coursify's autonomous agents take over. They scan verified web sources in real time, compile the most relevant and up-to-date information, and organize it into a guided course structure complete with modules, sections, and internal cross-references. No hallucinated facts — every claim is grounded in live research.

**3. Read, explore, and get assessed**

Your finished course opens in the interactive reader. Navigate between modules, read rich Markdown-formatted prose, follow inline LaTeX math formulas, and inspect syntax-highlighted code snippets. When you're ready to test your understanding, launch the AI-generated multiple-choice quizzes built specifically for your course material.

## Two generation modes

Coursify offers two modes to match your time and depth requirements.

| | **Flash** | **Research** |
|---|---|---|
| **Speed** | Seconds to a minute | Deeper crawl, may take a few minutes |
| **Engine** | Standard web research | Deep-Research Pro model |
| **Best for** | Quick overviews, topic surveys | Thorough study, complex subjects |
| **Availability** | Free (5/month) & Pro | Pro plan |

<Note>
  Flash generations count toward your monthly quota. Free accounts receive **5 Flash generations per month**. Pro unlocks unlimited generations with the deeper Research engine.
</Note>

## No account required to browse

All public courses on Coursify are free to read — no sign-in, no paywall. Visit [coursify.hasanraiyan.me/courses](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/courses) to browse the full library of community-generated courses at any time. You can also explore standalone deep-dive research articles in the [Research section](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/research), all freely accessible without an account.

To **generate and save your own courses**, you need a free Coursify account. Creating one takes under a minute and immediately unlocks your generation quota, personal course library, and quiz history.
