This page walks you through everything you need to go from zero to your first fully generated course on Coursify. You'll browse existing public courses, create a free account, pick a generation mode, submit a topic, and start learning — all in under two minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse without an account">
    Before you sign up, take a look at what Coursify produces. Head to [coursify.hasanraiyan.me/courses](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/courses) to browse the full library of public courses — every one of them is free to read with no sign-in required.

    Open any course to preview the interactive reader, explore the module structure, and get a feel for the depth and formatting Coursify generates. Courses cover subjects from Operating Systems and Compiler Design to System Design, Machine Learning, and beyond.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a free account">
    When you're ready to generate your own courses, go to [coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/sign-up) and create a free account.

    Signing up gives you:
    - **5 Flash course generations per month** at no cost
    - A personal course library to save and revisit your generated courses
    - Quiz history and progress tracking across all your courses

    No credit card is required to create a free account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a generation mode">
    Coursify gives you two generation modes. Pick the one that fits your goal:

    | Mode | Speed | Engine | Availability |
    |---|---|---|---|
    | **Flash** | Seconds to ~1 min | Standard web research | Free (5/mo) & Pro |
    | **Research** | A few minutes | Deep-Research Pro model | Pro plan |

    Use **Flash** when you want a quick, well-structured overview of a subject. Switch to **Research** when you're studying something complex and need maximum depth, source coverage, and analytical rigor.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your topic">
    In the course generator, type any topic or paste in a university syllabus. Coursify handles both:

    - **Single topics** — e.g., `"Operating Systems"`, `"Basics of Machine Learning"`, `"React hooks"`, `"Quantum Computing for Beginners"`, `"History of the Roman Empire"`
    - **University syllabuses** — paste the full text of a module outline or exam syllabus and Coursify will structure a course around it

    The more specific your topic, the more targeted your course. `"Sorting Algorithms in Python"` will produce a tighter, more focused result than just `"Algorithms"`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for AI generation">
    After you submit, Coursify's autonomous agents get to work:

    1. **Research** — agents scan verified web sources in real time to gather up-to-date, accurate information on your topic
    2. **Outline** — the AI organizes findings into a logical module-by-module structure
    3. **Write** — each section is written in full, with rich Markdown formatting, LaTeX math where relevant, and code snippets for technical topics

    Flash generations typically complete in seconds to about a minute. Research-mode generations take a few minutes depending on topic complexity. You'll see a progress indicator while generation is underway.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start learning">
    Once generation is complete, your course opens in the interactive reader. From here you can:

    - **Navigate modules** using the course sidebar to jump between sections
    - **Read with rich formatting** — prose, LaTeX math, syntax-highlighted code, and hoverable keyword definitions all render inline
    - **Take quizzes** — launch AI-generated multiple-choice assessments built specifically for your course to test your retention
    - **Track progress** — Coursify marks sections as you complete them so you can pick up where you left off
    - **Share or export** — copy a public link to share your course, or export to Markdown or PDF on Pro plans
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to your dashboard">
    Every course you generate is automatically saved to your personal dashboard. Visit your [dashboard](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/dashboard) at any time to:

    - **Browse your saved courses** and re-open any past generation
    - **Review your generation history** — see every course and research article you've created
    - **Pick up where you left off** — progress is tracked per section so you can resume a course immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Free accounts include **5 Flash generations per month**. Generations reset at the start of each billing cycle. If you need unlimited generations or access to the deep Research engine, check out the [Pro plan](/billing/plans).
</Note>

<Tip>
  Don't want a full course? You can also generate standalone **research articles** — shorter, source-backed deep-dives on any topic. Select the Research article option from the generator, or browse the public library at [coursify.hasanraiyan.me/research](https://coursify.hasanraiyan.me/research). Research articles are free to read and great for quick reference before committing to a full course generation.
</Tip>
