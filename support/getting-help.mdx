If you run into a problem with your account, course generation, saved provider keys, or anything else on Coursify, this page explains how to reach support and what to include in your request so we can help you as quickly as possible.

## Contact support

Send an email to [raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com](mailto:raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com) to reach the Coursify support team. To help us respond faster, include:

- The email address connected to your Coursify account
- A short description of the issue you're experiencing
- Screenshots, error messages, or links to relevant courses when helpful

Priority is given to account access, billing, privacy, and security requests.

## Response time

Coursify is an independent product built and maintained by a small team. We respond to support requests as quickly as possible. Account access, billing, privacy, and security requests are handled with priority. If your request is urgent, say so in your email subject line.
