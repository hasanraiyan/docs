If you want to delete your Coursify account or any personal data associated with it, this page explains exactly what can be deleted, how to submit a deletion request, and what to expect during processing. Read through the steps below before submitting your request.

**Last updated: June 14, 2026**

## What you can delete

You can request deletion of the following data associated with your Coursify account:

- **Account profile and authentication data** — your Coursify account profile and all authentication-linked application data.
- **Courses and content** — courses, modules, sections, research notes, generation history, and related drafts you have created.
- **Saved keys and configuration** — provider API keys, tool keys, and user-specific settings saved in your account.
- **Waitlist records** — any waitlist registrations or early-access records associated with your email address.

  </Step>

## Verification

We may ask you to verify that you own the account before we delete any data. This step protects you from unauthorized deletion requests submitted by someone else. Respond to any verification message promptly to avoid delays in processing your request.

## Processing time

We aim to process all deletion requests within 30 days of verification. Some records — such as entries in security logs, backup systems, or fraud-prevention records — may remain temporarily beyond that period where retention is required for operational, legal, or security reasons. These residual records are not used for any active purpose and are removed when retention periods expire.

<Warning>
  **Deletion is permanent.** Once your account and data are deleted, they cannot be recovered. Export or save any course content, research notes, or other material you want to keep **before** you submit a deletion request.
</Warning>

## Before deleting

Take a moment to save anything you want to keep:

- Copy or export course content and research notes from the Coursify reader before submitting your request.
- Export to Markdown or PDF if you have a Pro plan. See [Export and Rich Reader](/features/rich-reader) for options.
- Download or record any saved provider key values if you want to reuse them with other services.

Once you submit your deletion request and it is processed, none of this data can be retrieved.
