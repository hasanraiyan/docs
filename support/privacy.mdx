This policy explains what information Coursify collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you as a user. Coursify helps you generate, organize, and read AI-assisted learning content. We collect only the information needed to provide the service, protect accounts, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

**Last updated: June 14, 2026**

## Information we collect

When you use Coursify, we may collect the following categories of information:

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and authentication identifiers created when you sign up or sign in.
- **Course and content data** — courses, modules, sections, research notes, prompts, and document content you create or import into Coursify.
- **Usage information** — generation history, credit usage, feature interactions, and error logs that help us operate and improve the product.
- **Device and technical information** — browser type, IP address, and performance data collected automatically when you use the service.
- **Provider API keys** — optional keys you save to enable bring-your-own-key integrations for AI or other connected providers.

## How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your Coursify account.
- Generate courses, research summaries, quizzes, and related learning content on your behalf.
- Store your drafts, published courses, usage history, and account settings.
- Prevent abuse, enforce usage limits, troubleshoot issues, and keep the service secure.
- Communicate with you about support requests, product updates, waitlist status, and service changes.

## AI providers and subprocessors

Coursify may send your prompts, source material, and generated content to AI, search, storage, analytics, authentication, and infrastructure providers that help operate the product. These providers process data only for the service functions they support and are not authorized to use your data for their own purposes.

## User API keys

When you save provider keys in Coursify, we store them to enable the integrations you have selected. Your keys are used only for the features you activate. You can remove saved provider keys from your account settings at any time.

## Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers** — with vendors and infrastructure partners that help us operate Coursify, subject to appropriate data protection agreements.
- **Legal requirements** — when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
- **Protection of rights** — to protect the rights, property, or safety of Coursify, its users, or the public.
- **Business transfers** — as part of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to affected users.

## Data retention

We keep your account and content data while your account is active or as otherwise needed to provide the service. Some logs and backups may be retained for a limited period for security, reliability, legal, or fraud-prevention reasons, even after you delete content or close your account.

## Your choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

- **Edit or delete content** — you can edit or delete your own courses and research content directly within the product.
- **Remove provider keys** — you can remove saved provider API keys from your account settings at any time.
- **Request account deletion** — you can request deletion of your account and associated data. See the [Data Deletion](/support/data-deletion) page for instructions.
- **Contact support** — email [raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com](mailto:raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com) for questions about access, correction, export, or deletion of your information.

## Children

Coursify is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
